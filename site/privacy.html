Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 2, 2026

Overview

GACE Scan is a market analytics app that scans market data and organizes attention signals for educational use. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

Information We Collect

We may collect account information you provide, such as your email address, phone number, display name, notification preferences, watchlist symbols, subscription status, app diagnostics, and basic device information needed to operate the service.

Market Data and Watchlists

GACE Scan may process symbols, watchlists, signal interactions, and alert preferences to personalize scanning, reminders, and app behavior. We do not need your brokerage password or bank login to provide scanner features.

Notifications

If you enable notifications, we may use your device notification token and time zone to deliver alerts, morning summaries, and scanner reminders.

Payments

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your full payment card number from Apple.

How We Use Information

We use information to operate the app, personalize scans and alerts, improve reliability, provide support, manage subscriptions, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers that help us run the app, process analytics, provide notifications, or comply with law.

Financial Disclaimer

GACE Scan provides educational market analytics only. It is not financial advice, investment advice, or a recommendation to buy, sell, or hold any security or asset.

Data Retention

We keep information only as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal requirements, resolve disputes, and improve product reliability.

Your Choices

You can disable notifications in iOS Settings. You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID. You may contact us to request support with account or privacy questions.
