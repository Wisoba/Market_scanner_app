Common Questions

Does GACE Scan tell me what to buy or sell?

No. GACE Scan provides educational market analytics and attention signals. It does not provide financial advice or guaranteed outcomes.

Why do some signals expire?

Market attention changes quickly. Expiration helps keep stale signals from looking current.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID in the App Store subscription settings.

How do I report a bug?

Email support@gacescan.com and include screenshots if possible.
